Two minutes of reading. No paperwork required.

What's happening

One of the people working here takes part in Instawork Robotics Lab — a program that pays workers to wear a small camera during their shift. The footage is used to teach AI and robots how real work gets done.

They've asked for your okay to record while they do their job here. The camera captures their own point of view as they work. It is not a security system and it is not there to monitor your staff or your customers.

What you control

  • It only happens if you say yes. Nothing starts without your okay.
  • You decide what's off-limits — any area, any time. Just tell them.
  • You can ask them to pause or stop on the spot, for any reason.
  • They start and stop the camera themselves while doing their normal work.
  • Nothing is required from you: no equipment, no cost, no setup.

What you might get out of it

Most managers do this simply as a favor to a good worker, and that's completely fine. If recording at a business like yours turns out to be valuable to us, our team may reach out about a paid, more formal partnership later on. Letting us know you're open (below) puts you on our radar first — but it's optional.
